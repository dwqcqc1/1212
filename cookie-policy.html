Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Burgos and Brein Wealth Management (“Burgos and Brein LLC,” “we,” or “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, burgosandbrein.com. This policy should be read in conjunction with our comprehensive Privacy Policy.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your computer or mobile device when you visit them. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, improve user experience, and provide website owners with analytics about site usage.

Types of Cookies We Use
  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website’s basic functionality, such as enabling secure login areas or remembering your preferences. The website cannot function properly without them.
  • Performance Cookies: These collect information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit frequently and how long you stay. This data helps us understand user behavior and improve website functionality.
  • Marketing Cookies: These cookies may be placed by us or third-parties to track your browsing activity across websites. This enables us to deliver more relevant advertising and content that aligns with your interests.
Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your web browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See a list of cookies stored on your device
  • Delete individual or all cookies
  • Block cookies by default
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Please note that disabling cookies may hinder certain features and functionalities of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may use third-party services that employ cookies, such as analytics tools or advertising platforms. These third-parties are responsible for their own cookie practices. Please refer to their individual privacy policies for more information.
