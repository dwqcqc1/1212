About Us
Burgos & Brein Wealth Management, based in Orlando, specializes in assisting veterans through VA Aid & Attendance claims, long-term life planning, retirement planning, and Medicare. With over a decade of experience, our team combines empathy with expertise to ensure the community and its loved ones are prepared and secure for the future.

About Burgos & Brein Wealth Management
Founding Principles
Founded by Gabriel Burgos, Burgos & Brein Wealth Management is driven by a passion for providing comprehensive, unbiased financial services. Our firm prioritizes understanding our clients’ needs, fostering trust, delivering personalized service, and developing strategies aligned with their unique goals. Our principles reflect a firm commitment to their financial success.
Our Philosophy
We believe every client deserves guidance tailored to their life’s journey. We focus on building long-term relationships through integrity, transparency, and empathy, offering strategies that protect, preserve, and grow wealth for generations.
Our Commitment
Our commitment is to be a steadfast partner to our clients and their families. We are dedicated to their prosperity, providing education, proactive communication, and responsive support through every stage of retirement and care planning.
Our Expertise
With years of experience in VA Aid & Attendance, Medicaid, retirement, and investment planning, our team delivers specialized solutions. We stay current with the latest regulations and financial tools to provide the highest standard of service.
