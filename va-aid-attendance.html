About VA Aid & Attendance

VA Aid & Attendance is a benefit available to qualifying veterans and their surviving spouses to help cover the cost of care. At Burgos & Brein Wealth Management, we specialize in assisting you to navigate eligibility and the application process so you can access the benefits you deserve.

Eligibility Requirements for VA Aid & Attendance

To qualify for VA Aid & Attendance, applicants must meet specific service, health, and income criteria. Our team can help you determine your eligibility and guide you through the process.

  • Wartime Veteran (served at least 90 days active duty, with at least one day during wartime)
  • Surviving spouse of a wartime veteran
  • Demonstrated need for assistance with daily living activities or are housebound
  • Meet financial and medical requirements

Detailed Overview of VA Aid & Attendance Benefits

The Aid & Attendance benefit can help cover a variety of care costs. Eligible applicants can receive monthly payments to assist with in-home care, assisted living, or nursing home expenses.

  • Assistance with daily activities (bathing, dressing, eating, etc.)
  • Home care and assisted living support
  • Skilled nursing facility care
  • Housebound benefits for those with limited mobility
  • Surviving spouses support

Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

Applying for VA Aid & Attendance can be complex. Avoid these frequent mistakes:

  • Not meeting eligibility criteria
  • Incomplete or inaccurate application forms
  • Missing or insufficient supporting documentation
  • Delays in submission
  • Failure to track application status

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the VA Aid & Attendance benefit?
The VA Aid & Attendance benefit provides financial assistance to veterans and their surviving spouses who need help with daily living activities.
Who qualifies for Aid & Attendance?
Wartime veterans, their surviving spouses, and those who require assistance with daily activities may qualify, subject to financial and medical requirements.
How long does the application process take?
The process can take several months. Our team can help ensure your application is complete to help minimize delays.
What documents do I need for my application?
Service records, medical documentation, financial statements, and VA forms are typically required.
